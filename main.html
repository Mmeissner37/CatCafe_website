Frosted Whiskers

Cat Café and Pâtisserie

Welcome fellow cat lovers!

Located in the Shenandoah Valley, we are the first cat café in the area! We work with local feline rescues, such as SPCA NOVA, Dakota’s Dream Animal Rescue and Frederick County Esther Boyd Animal Shelter, to house adoptable cats in need of homes.

You'll find a delicious menu of coffee and homemade pastries, along with a selection of products to enrich your feline friend's life. Each purchase provides financial support for our cats. Extra proceeds are donated to the local rescues we work with.
